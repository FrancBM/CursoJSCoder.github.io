RECEIVING PROCESS

Receive and verify

Check that the order that has been received is complete and that they are the products that were requested. Also, check that you agree with the data requested in the Purchase Order.

03-01-2022

Good Receipt and IVT

Carry out the Good Receipt on the Webuy platform and request the respective invoice from the supplier to upload it to the IVT platform.

04-01-2022

Register

Once the invoice is uploaded to the IVT platform, wait for it to appear on the platform and carry out the respective approval. Then register the products on this web page.

06-01-2022

Fixed assets

If the products are fixed assets, do not forget to send an email to the finance area so that they activate it and generate their respective ASSETS numbers.
